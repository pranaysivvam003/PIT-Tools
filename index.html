Process Improvement Tools
Alt-Tag

⚡ Alt-Tag Tool - Guide
Drag the Alt-Tag button to your bookmarks → open your HTML email → click the bookmark to show ALT text → click again to bring images back.

Ids QA

⚡ IDs QA Tool - Guide
Drag the “IDs QA” button to your bookmarks → open your email HTML → click the bookmark to display all link ID values → click again to return the page to its original state.

Character Converter

⚡ Character Converter – Task Flow Giude
Drag the Character Converter button to your bookmarks → paste your code into the left container → click Convert → view the decoded special characters in the right container.

Text Comparison

⚡ Text Comparator – Task Flow Guide
Drag the Text Comparator button to your bookmarks → enter Version 1 (original text) and Version 2 (corrected text) → click Compare → the tool highlights differences and outputs the corrected final text.
